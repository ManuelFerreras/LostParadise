Introduction

Intro

Lost Paradise(LP) is a play-to-earn game deployed on the Binance Smart Chain(BSC). The game will possess a tycoon-type gameplay and an in-game economy with its own utility token(LPS), which will have a real-world value and that can be exchanged for other cryptocurrencies.

Our vision and Goals

Our goal is to establish a solid and trustworthy play-to-earn game for people who are looking for confidence and security for their investment. Furthermore, we aim to develop a game where players can not only earn money but at the same time clear their mind and leave all the stress and chaos of their daily lives behind.

Gameplay

Buildings

Buildings are going to be the main asset in the game, they can be either purchased in the in-game shop, traded in the marketplace, or obtained while combining two of them.

Building Parts, Types and Combinations

There are going to be 4 different types of buildings:

  • House
  • Hotel
  • Market
  • Dojo

Every single one of the buildings will be formed by 4 parts:

  • Ceiling
  • Body
  • Door
  • Foundation

The body is the only part that defines what type the building is going to be. Thus, a building can be of one type and at the same time possess one or more parts that are from another type of building. Furthermore all the parts differ in aesthetics and qualities.

Buildings Levels and Upgrades

Additionally to the uniqueness and the parts, a building will possess another attribute: the level. A building's level will have no correlation with its parts and uniqueness.



While most of the building's attributes are static (once they are minted they can´t change), the level is not. For this it is only necessary to purchase the upgrade with LPS.

Production and Storage

There are 2 importants denotations that every player must understand to start playing Lost Paradise: Production and Storage.



The production is the quantity of LPS Token a building can generate until its storage is full. The measurement of the production will be per hour (LPS/per hour).



The storage is the quantity of LPS a building can stock. Once their building storage is full, players will have to collect the tokens they have produced in order to let their buildings continue with their production.

Important: each one of the 4 parts will add or subtract the percentage shown in the table. Example: a full house will have its production reduced by 40% (-10x4) while its storage is augmented by 40% (10x4).

Slots

Slots will be an item that can only be bought in the in-game shop, they serve to settle the buildings to start earning LPS Token.

Gambling

The gamble system will let players bet LPS in order to obtain high profits. There will be 2 different team-based gambles where the team with LESS votes wins. It will be possible to bet only 1 time in each one of these bets per account, and the quantity of LPS someone can place is fixed with 4 different options.



Each one of these options will count as a different number of votes, the higher the bet, the higher the votes it represents.



The gambles and the fixed options are the following:

  • 1500 LPS = 1 VOTE
  • 2500 LPS = 2 VOTES
  • 5000 LPS = 4 VOTES
  • 10000 LPS = 8 VOTES

2 Teams bets:

In the 2 teams gamble, there will be a time span of 1 day where players can vote for one of the 2 teams. The team with less votes will be the winner. Winners will get 190% of the money they initially put. In case there is a draw all the participants will obtain 90% of their money.



4 Teams bets:

In the 4 teams gamble, there will be a time span of 3 days where players can vote for one of the 4 teams. The team with less votes will be the winner. winners will get 350% of the money they initially put. In case there is a draw all the participants will obtain 90% of their money.

Marketplace and Shop

Marketplace Function

Lost paradise marketplace will be the place where players will be able to trade freely their own collectibles(NFT)with other players. For each sale transaction there will be a 2.5% fee which will go directly to the owner's wallet.

In-Game Shop

Additionally to the marketplace, the game will have an in-game shop, where it will be possible to purchase Slots or mint a new random building. The shop is going to have a fee of 10% that will go 90% to the rewards pool and 10% to the devs wallet

Play-to-Earn

What is Play to Earn?


Currently, play-to-earn games are becoming more and more popular among gamers. But what is play-to-earn? and how do these games work?.

Play-to-earn is a game model that allow users to collect crypto tokens and NFTs that can be sold on the market. It gives players the chance to earn crypto just by playing their favourite games.


There are typically 3 different ways of earning money in a play-to-earn game:


  • Earning crypto by playing
  • Trading NTFs
  • Staking
  • Play-to-earn has created in-game economies where players can play for an income

How to Earn Money in the Game

In Lost Paradise it will be possible to earn money by owning and managing buildings. The buildings will generate a certain amount of LPS based on the level, parts, rareness, and the type of slot where it is placed. In order to start producing LPS, buildings must be placed in a Slot.


When buildings produce LPS there is an early claim fee of 25% in 5 days. When a day has passed, the fee will lower 5% until the 5 days period is over. In this way, we will encourage the long term economy of the game.


For a more effective production it is recommended to pay attention to the game so as not to let the building fill their storage. If this happens, buildings will cease their production.

Tokenomics

Token

The primary currency of Lost Paradise is going to be LPS Token.

LPS is a token deployed on the BSC network and it is exclusively used for in-game transactions. Outside the game it can be used for trading in different DEXs.

Token Distribution

The total token supply is going to be 1.000.000.000 $LPS

Distribution:

  • 2.5% owners (25.000.000 LPS)
  • 2.5% expenses (25.000.000 LPS)
  • 5% prívate sale (50.000.000 LPS)
  • 10% public sale (100.000.000LPS)
  • 80% in-game & staking rewards pool (800.000.000 LPS)

For the owners the 2.5% of LPS will be unlocked in a total period of 12 months.
